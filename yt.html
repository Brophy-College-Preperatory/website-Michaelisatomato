Michaelisatomato: The YouTube Channel

Michaelisatomato started around April, with the theme of being a "Friendly Fortnite Player". He posted videos of giving his teammates' crowns, and accumulated many subscribers. Michael consistently posted until around June, when he had to slow down uploads due to SHEP. However, his channel blossomed in July, when he posted "The Rare Chest Ritual". He has consistently posted and has accumulated over 12,000 subscribers!
