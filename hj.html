Hamra Jewelers: The Family Business

Hamra Jewelers is a family jewelry store. The main focus is watches, with many notable brands such as Rolex and Patek Phillipe. There is aslo a good selection of other jewelry, such as necklaces, rings, and bracelets. The store is moving to a bigger location, and the grand opening is likely going to be in 2 weeks! This has been in the works for almost 3 years, and is almost a complete project!
